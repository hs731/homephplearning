Our Origin

The history of out coffee and it's recipe dates all the way back to the 15th century where Kaldi discovered coffee beans and it's usage when eating berries near an arabica tree. From that point Kaldi invented Coffee and opened the shop named Coffee Time. Now in 2019, we are still continuing his legacy with fair trade coffee beans.

Quality

Through time we have maintained our coffee taste and made sure we never lost the quality of our great tasting coffee. Quality is important us at Coffee Time.
